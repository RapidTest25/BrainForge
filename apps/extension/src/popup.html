Flow

The intended flow is simple now:

  1. Open BrainForge Meetings on the website
  2. Let the website sync the extension session automatically
  3. Join Google Meet and launch the assistant from the call controls
  4. Generate the summary and review the meeting back in BrainForge
Tip: if the website shows an older extension version, reload the unpacked extension in `chrome://extensions/`.
